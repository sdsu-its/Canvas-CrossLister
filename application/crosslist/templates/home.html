Instructions

To get started, you will need your API key:

  1. Go to your Canvas account settings.
  2. Hover over to the Approved Integrations section.
  3. Click on Generate New Access Token.
  4. Follow the Canvas Course Naming Guideline.

After generating the API key and revewing the guideline, you are ready to Crosslist!
